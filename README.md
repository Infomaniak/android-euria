# Euria — A Sovereign AI Assistant

**Euria is a sovereign AI assistant developed by Infomaniak that respects your privacy and the planet.**

Use the power of AI without compromise: Euria protects your privacy, runs exclusively on renewable energy, and fully reuses the
heat it generates to warm homes.

Euria can be used for free with a my kSuite account.

## What you can do with Euria

- Submit requests in writing or by voice
- Perform smart web searches
- Write, translate, proofread, summarize, or enhance texts and documents
- Transcribe recordings and audio files
- Extract or analyze text from PDF, Word, Excel, and other files
- Interpret and describe images
- Find original and creative ideas
- And Euria is constantly evolving with new capabilities

## Why use Euria?

- **100%** hosted in Switzerland by Infomaniak
- **100%** powered by local renewable energy
- **100%** of the energy reused to heat homes
- **100%** compliant with GDPR and the Swiss Data Protection Act (LPD)
- **200%** CO₂ emissions offset
- Smart and energy-efficient search that automatically connects to the Internet when relevant

## Euria is secure and privacy-friendly

- No data used to train models: your data belongs to you
- Hosting and processing exclusively in Switzerland
- Conversations encrypted in transit and on our servers
- Ephemeral mode for conversations with no trace

## And much more

- Organize your conversations by project
- Access them across all your devices
- Share selected discussions with your contacts
